Rethinking training program pairing experience

Cléo Chatbot helps job seekers making career switches find the right training programs to successfully land a job.

  • 1 interactive demo
  • 6 user interviews
  • pitched to France Travail, a national public service for unemployed people

This PimpMyApp bootcamp experience allowed me to design a complete user interaction flow centered on gamification as part of our solution.

Challenge

Helping job seekers success in tech career transitions

France Travail faces high enrollment in tech training programs but struggles with completion rates.

This drop rate reduces job seekers employment prospects in the tech field.

Problem Mapping

Job seekers struggle understanding tech jobs' requirements

I conducted 6 interviews with job seekers in career transition to understand where they get stuck in the process.

Finding the right information on tech jobs

Through our interviews, I identified that job seekers lacked visibility on what tech roles actually involve: the day-to-day tasks, the skill levels required, and what it takes to be the right candidate.

Identifying the most relevant training

I also mapped a second pain point: job seekers had no way to gauge their chances of getting hired after completing a training program, making it hard to commit.

Mapping job seekers key steps into getting in a training program

Opportunity

How might we make finding relevant training in tech more efficient?

Hypothesis

If we let job seekers experience actual job, they will make more informed career decisions.

If we show local job market demand in real-time, users will be more invested in completing relevant programs.

Design Exploration

Understanding the job reality

I quickly prototyped around a developer's workflow and tested with 5 job seekers.

Initial feedback revealed poor engagement. Users were dissatisfied at only guessing required skills.

Job seekers need hands-on experience, not just skill lists. I pivoted to a coding website exercise.

Before: selecting skills required to complete a job's tasks

After: completing an immersive task based game with the help of the chatbot

Results

Measuring success

KPIs Ideas

  • % job seekers consulting training programs after completing the game
  • % job seekers enrolling in a training program relevant to the job they consulted
  • % job seekers completing training program
