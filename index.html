About Me

Designing less boring software

I came to product design after three years at Salesforce, where I learned what enterprise software actually costs its users.

Since then, I have been designing for users that have 7 screens open at the same time. I focus on legibility and on giving users the right information to trust the tools they rely on, without ever compromising on the fun, appealing side of design.
